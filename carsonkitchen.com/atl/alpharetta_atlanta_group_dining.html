Carson Kitchen Alpharetta Group Dining

Downtown Alpharetta's newest restaurant venue for hosting your wedding reception, rehersal dinner, birthday celebration, or casual business get-together

Carson Kitchen offers several choices for your private dining event in Downtown Alpharetta. When in season, our patio dining area is the perfect space for your intimate gathering of friends or business colleagues. The outdoor patio can seat up to 44 guests for dining and up to 80 guests standing. The main dining room can accommodate up to 80 guests for seated dining and up to 160 for standing. Groups may reserve either space, or both, should the guest count demand it.

Housed in a replica of the old 1848 Milton County courthouse, Carson Kitchen is located in Alpharetta City Center in the heart of downtown Alpharetta. No matter the occasion, Carson Kitchen will welcome you with open arms!

Large Party Menu Choices

No longer do guests have to commit to a preselected entrÉe

For any party planner the biggest challenge is choosing a menu that will appeal to all of their guests' tastes, dietary restrictions, and food allergies—we get it. Instead of forcing the guest to choose an item from "we've seen those entrée choices on every banquet menu before,” Chef provides table-center offerings of Carson Kitchen's most popular items. Guests can help themselves to one or all items. No more wishing you had ordered what your friend had… it's all shared, so everyone's happy.
